Intro and tutorial.

Yeet Wars is a game in a beta where you fight to the death.
The world has gone in to a apocalpyse and the resources are limited.
You need to knock your opponet off the ledge in order to eliminate them
so you can be the last person to survive. You have also been intoduced to
radioactivity so you have abilities.


Controls

Player 1

Arrow keys to move

Slash to attack

Shift to jump


Player 2

WASD to move

Q to attack

Space to jump


What to see next

At the current moment, you're pills so expect 3D characters, most likely human.
