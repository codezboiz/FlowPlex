Intro and tutorial.

Explorer's Realm is a strategy game that is still in a beta.
You are stuck in a strange dream that has set you in the past and
you need to survive. Move with the arrow keys and collect from the
areas that you have moved to. Go to the title screen and click tutorial
for more information


Controls

Arrow keys to move

E to collect materials


What to see next

At the current moment you can only collect food and water (the only things
that are useful), but eventually you should see using wood and stone (not in the game
yet) to build stuff. You should also get a chance to see a bigger map and more areas.
