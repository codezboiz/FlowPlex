Intro and tutorial.

4th Dimension is a game where things are different from the 3rd dimension
You have been sent to the 4th dimension, where everything is different.
There are portals, which some are bad and send you back, and some help you.
You also have to change directions in order to move to new places.


Controls

Player 1

Arrow keys to move

Up arrow to jump

1 and 2 to change directions
